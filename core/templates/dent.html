Dental Care

Oral health is critical to your pet’s overall well-being. We provide comprehensive dental services to prevent, diagnose and treat dental diseases.

Dental Exams

Routine dental check-ups to assess your pet’s oral health, look for early warning signs and identify any issues before it gets too serious.

Professional Cleanings

Thorough cleaning under anesthesia to remove plaque, tartar, and prevent periodontal disease.

Extractions

Safe removal of damaged or diseased teeth to relieve pain and prevent infection.

Oral Surgery

Treatment of oral tumors, injuries, and other complex dental conditions.

Dental Care Advice

General guidance on brushing, diet, and products to maintain your pet’s oral health in-between visits.
